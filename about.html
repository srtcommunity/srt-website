Sagar Umaria
Founder & Head Educator, Smart Retail Traders

Smart Retail Traders was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between retail traders and the practical market knowledge traditionally accessible only to professionals. Sagar Umaria established SRT to create a learning ecosystem where traders receive structured education, actionable strategies, and ongoing mentorship designed for real-world market conditions.

With years of experience studying and navigating financial markets, Sagar has developed a teaching methodology centered on clarity, simplicity, and execution. His focus extends beyond theory, emphasizing the practical skills that traders need to identify opportunities, manage risk effectively, and build long-term consistency in the markets.

At SRT, every concept, framework, and trading model is taught through the lens of actual market behavior. Rather than relying solely on textbook knowledge, Sagar equips students with a deeper understanding of market structure, price action, trading psychology, and disciplined decision-making. His mission is to help traders develop the confidence and competence required to navigate today's financial markets independently.

"Successful trading isn't about finding a secret strategy—it's about understanding the market, managing risk, and executing consistently. My goal is to help traders build a process they can trust for years to come."

— Sagar

Today, Smart Retail Traders has become a growing community of aspiring and active traders from across India. Through personalized mentorship, practical market education, and a results-focused approach, Sagar continues to guide students toward becoming confident, self-reliant market participants. His commitment remains unchanged: delivering education that empowers traders to make informed decisions and achieve sustainable growth in their trading journey.
