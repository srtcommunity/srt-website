About the Course
What SRT teaches, how it's structured, and who it's designed for
Absolutely. The SRT curriculum starts from the very beginning with no prior market knowledge assumed. We take you through everything from understanding what a stock is, all the way to executing trades using advanced institutional-level SMC concepts. Beginners make up a large portion of every batch and consistently see strong results.
The course covers six markets in total: Indian Equity (NSE & BSE), Futures & Options (F&O), Forex, and Cryptocurrency. We also teach Smart Money Concepts (SMC) and ICT methodology — frameworks that apply across all of these markets, so you're not starting from scratch with each one.
The course runs as a live online cohort — you attend sessions in real-time with the full batch, ask questions live, and participate in live market analysis. Every session is also recorded and made available to you with lifetime access, so you never miss anything and can revisit any topic at your own pace.

The curriculum includes advanced modules on SMC and ICT concepts, market psychology, and specialised trading that go significantly beyond what most intermediate traders have been exposed to. Many of our students who join as intermediates say the SMC module and the psychology content are the most transformative parts of the programme.
Smart Money Concepts (SMC) is a broad framework for understanding how institutional traders (banks, hedge funds) accumulate and distribute positions. ICT (Inner Circle Trader) is a specific methodology within the SMC framework developed by Michael Huddleston, covering precise concepts like order blocks, fair value gaps, kill zones, and optimal trade entries. At SRT, we teach both and show how they work together.

During the Course
What to expect once you're enrolled and the cohort is live
All live sessions are held on Zoom. You'll receive the session link via WhatsApp before each class. You can attend from any device — phone, tablet, or computer. A stable internet connection is recommended for the best experience.
Every student receives curated PDF notes, reference sheets, and study materials corresponding to each module. These are shared during the course and serve as your ongoing trading reference library — covering everything from candlestick pattern cheat sheets to SMC concept summaries.
Doubt clearing happens through a dedicated channel. You can raise questions during live sessions in real-time, or submit them between sessions via the student group. Sagar reviews and addresses all doubts — typically within 24 hours of submission. No question goes unanswered.
Every live session is recorded and shared with all enrolled students, typically within a few hours of the session ending. You'll never fall behind — you can catch up at your own pace, and the recordings are available to you for lifetime access even after the cohort ends.
The course teaches you frameworks, analysis, and strategies. Live trade setups and market analysis are demonstrated during sessions to show real-world application. We do not make trades on your behalf or manage your money — the goal is to build your own skills and judgment, not create dependency.
